Information We Collect (and Why)

OCommute is a transit app, and its core features depend on location data. We are very clear about when and how we use this data.

  • Location Data (While App is in Use): We collect your precise location (using GPS and network signals) only when the app is open and visible on your screen. We use this to provide our main features: finding nearby stops, planning trips from your current location, and showing you real-time transit information.
  • [OPTIONAL - DELETE IF NOT USED] Location Data (In the Background): If you enable features like "Get-Off Alerts," we will ask for permission to track your location in the background (when the app is closed). We use this *only* to monitor your trip and send you a notification when you are approaching your stop. This data is not used for any other purpose.
  • Analytics Data (Non-Personal): We collect anonymous analytics data, such as app crashes and feature usage statistics. This helps us find and fix bugs and understand what features are most popular. This data is aggregated and cannot be used to identify you.
